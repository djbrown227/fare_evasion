The Future of MTA Buses

NYC’s bus system is at a tipping point. With ridership in flux, fare evasion climbing, and technology rapidly evolving, how can we reimagine the bus system to be faster, fairer, and more financially sustainable? This 3-part series explores the crisis—and possible future—of MTA buses through deep data analysis, policy breakdowns, and case studies from around the world.

An inside look at fare evasion, service performance, and why the system is breaking down—fueled by the latest MTA data.
